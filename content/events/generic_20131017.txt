Organised by the <span class="highlight">[RDP](http://www.strath.ac.uk/rdp/)</span>, the Postgraduate Induction is a full-day event for all new postgraduate research students.
Places can be booked through the <span class="highlight">[online booking system](https://bookings.strath.ac.uk/courseDetails.asp?course=PGRI)</span>.
Following the induction, PGRS will be running a <span class="highlight">[pub quiz](drink_20131017.html)</span> and opportunity for you to meet your fellow postgraduates in the Union.
