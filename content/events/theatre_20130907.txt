As part of the BBC's Last Night of the Proms, a variety of musical performers will be in Glasgow Green to perform live, and we're planning on being there.
Well, nearly there.

Rather than join the main crowd, our ex-president has kindly offered up his flat and garden.
Which'll basically be the same, but cheaper and with easier access to food and drink*.
If the weather's dry and the wind's good we should be able to hear it all from there.
If not, well then we can sit inside and put it on the TV instead, while laughing at everyone who paid to sit in the rain.
Or something.

Let us know if you're coming by signing up on <span class="highlight">[Facebook](https://www.facebook.com/events/510022235751174/)</span>.
